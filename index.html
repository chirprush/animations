Math Animations

Hello! My name is Rushil, and I'm really passionate about math and computer science. One of the little niches that I think really best combines these two subjects is making appealing-looking, clean animations of mathematical topics. As such, here is my current collection of math animations. I hope to make a lot more in the future!

  • Bezier Curves: A fun little animation showing off Bezier curves running through random points.
  • Unit Circle Waves: A classic animation showing off the two waves of the unit circle.
  • Simplices: With my journey into topic of simplices, I thought I would take the opportunity to make an animation projecting 4d simplices and looking at their rotations.
  • Graph Iterations: A cool looking (in my opinion at least) visualization of a cute little bijection between the natural numbers and graphs from graph theory.
